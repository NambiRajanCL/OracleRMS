6. Health
I ask about your health in advance as I represent multiple insurance companies and would like to ensure that we submit an application with the company that best suits your needs and personal situation. Rates are based on your age, smoking status, lifestyle and overall health, and each insurance company has different underwriting requirements. Providing me with the most accurate information about your health will also help me provide you with more accurate quotes, instead of assuming standard rates.

  • Has anyone ever explained the difference between term insurance and permanent life insurance?

  • How much overall debt do you have (see DEBT section above)?
  • Do you have savings that could easily be used and accessed at the time of death?
  • Would you like to also include funding for your childrens post-secondary college education or other long-term goals?

  • Would you like to include the cost of funeral costs and/or final expenses?
  • Do you have a monthly or annual budget?

  • Occupation - % of time spent on your various job duties?

  • Are you covered by WSIB?
